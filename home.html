Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk in the egg, milk, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Heat a skillet and pour batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup and enjoy!

Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and sauté the onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in curry powder, tomatoes, and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and beat until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool and frost as desired.

Simple Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes (diced or whole)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, until fragrant.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes (with juices) to the pan and bring to a simmer. Cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Once the pasta is done, drain it and add it to the sauce. Toss to combine.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
